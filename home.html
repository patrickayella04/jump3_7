BRAZILIAN JIU JITSU

Established in 2010, Ilford Academia de Jiu Jitsu has gone from strength to strength in providing quality martial arts in East London (Ilford). Our classes are suitable for experienced martial artists and beginners, alike.

Our Jiu Jitsu is based on Grandmaster Helio Gracie's original fight philosophies:

  • Energy efficiency
  • Punch proof technique
  • All techniques are based on natural body movement

We teach the original and complete Gracie Jiu Jitsu standing self defence programme as our core fundamentals. Weapons defence, Striking, Clinch work, Throwing and Groundwork.

Professor Dave Birkett took his first martial arts class back in 1976 (Judo), his first Gracie Jiu Jitsu Class in 1996, receiving his Black Belt from Professor Marc Walder (3rd degree) back in 2012 and is currently a 2nd degree Black belt.

Professor Dave also holds the following ranks:

  • Combat Submission Wrestling Coach (2002) under Sensei Erik Paulson
  • Jun Fan Gung Fu Instructor under Guro Dan Inosanto
  • Fillipino Martial Arts Instructor under Guro Dan Inosanto
  • 2nd Degree Black belt in JKD under Guro Bob Breen
  • Full Instructor in Integrated Arts (JKD, Boxing, Muay Thai, Kali) under Guro Terry Barnett
  • 1st Dan Black Belt in Shotokan Karate (Silver Medalist in Southern England 1989)
  • Gracie Combatives (98% pass) BBS1 (97% pass) under Ryron and Rener Gracie

Professor Marc was the first UK student to be certified by the Gracie Academy (Grandmasters Helio, Rorion & Royce Gracie) back in 2000. Marc has been a long time Student and friend of Royce Gracie whom also took Private lessons with and was promoted by, Grand master Helio Gracie himself. Marc continued his studies, competing all over the world and was promoted to Black Belt by Rolls Gracie legend Mestre Mauricio Gomes in 2005.
